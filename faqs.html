Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to join?

As shared on the main page, the fellowship, and all its resources will be completely free of cost.

How long does the outreach run?

The core outreach begins with a short, focused series of ten lectures on atomic structure, followed by guest seminars. Beyond the series, we keep materials and recordings available so students can continue learning at their own pace.

Who is eligible to participate?

We prioritise motivated students from low-income or under-resourced backgrounds who show curiosity about chemistry. That said, anyone with a genuine interest in learning is welcome to express interest, and we look forward to hearing from you!

How much prior knowledge of Chemistry is required?

We recommend high school chemistry knowledge, although we will start from the basics and build up from there.

Will the lectures and materials be publicly available??

Yes, all lectures and materials will be publicly available. We will also provide access to the recorded lecture library, slides, and invited-seminar recordings.

This seems very exciting, can I help?

Yes! Please contact us at vihaanagrawal@proton.me and we can see how you can help. Even if you cannot teach, there will almost surely be ways you can contribute in!

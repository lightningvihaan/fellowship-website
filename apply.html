Express interest to join our Chemistry outreach program

The Seaborg Fellowship runs a free, targeted outreach program for students with a strong curiosity for Chemistry.

If you want to express interest, orhave any queries or requests, feel free to reach out on our email: vihaanagrawal@proton.me

Selection & Support

This fellowship is open to all students who are interested in Chemistry. We are not looking for perfect scores. Instead, we look for curiosity, persistence, and a willingness to learn. Students who demonstrate consistent interest may be offered extra mentorship.

How to tell us about yourself

When you contact us, you don't need polished essays — simply tell us:

  • Your name, age, school, and preferred language for learning
  • Why you like Chemistry (one or two sentences is fine)
  • Any projects, experiments, or questions you've tried or wondered about
  • If you need help with internet or devices — we will try to look for alternatives

I'm now a Fellow! What next?

That's great! You will receive access to the recorded lecture library, slides, and invited-seminar recordings. We will also provide motivated students with guidance on how to start simple, safe projects or computational curiosities.

For teachers & community partners

If you work with students who might benefit from this program, please get in touch. We can provide slide decks,and suggest low-cost demonstrations you can run in class.

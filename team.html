Meet the Team

The Seaborg Fellowship is powered by a group of students and young researchers who are deeply passionate about Chemistry and education. Our team works to make research accessible, inspiring, and within reach for every curious learner — especially those from under-resourced backgrounds.

Our Experience

We believe that every student deserves the chance to see how science really works — through questions, experiments, and ideas that shape the world today.

Our organizers, instructors, and contributors bring experience from research labs, Olympiads(IChO, IMO, etc.), and mentorship programs to build that bridge.

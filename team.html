Meet the Team

We are a bunch of students who really like our subject and want to spread it further. Having previously participated in major competitions, we understand the challenges students in the community face and want to help them.

Our Experience

The team consists of many competition medalists, as well as others who have attended major training camps.

Many of us have not only excelled in competitions, but also done well on other exams like standardized tests and university entrance exams.
